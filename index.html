About CISC

The Culinary School of South Carolina was created in 1964 by Charles Downs whose goal was to create a environment where he could teach others about his passion for food. Due to his hard work and deicatication, Charles achieved his dream. As a result we have the outstanding Culinary Institute of South Carolina. Created by a dream itself, this school allows young chefs of South Carolina to shot for their wildest dreams. Providing opportunities to makes sure our students become the best chefs they can be. With the help of our highly talented staff, students are able to learn things out of the ordinary and get to really exploit the field of choice. Programs, from icing decoration classes to restaurant management classes, taught here on our campus grounds are ensured to spark the interest of our students and allows them to learn in an environment where their creativity is not denied but embraced by staff and the others surrounding them. Our Mission is to make sure that every student that comes through our programs, leaves with a different mind set then what they entered with, in hopes they learned something to carry forth with them.
